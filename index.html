The New Kids

1: On Something to Nothing

05 November 2022 | Sunday


Through nothing something was formed. Through something nothing was formed.

I have lately come to disdain extremism as in the above sentence even in poetry. Not only do they seem to make no sense they also seem quite powerful in propagating a contagious mentality of transactional actions demanding profit out of the simplest things. This mentality then seeps in the daily life and trivilises the fundamental concept of unconditional joy.

I would not be unwise to be sad to say that this mentality may have already seeped deep in our general social psyche. It , at this point, may even be so deep that the concept of unconditional joy may no more be a fundamental state everyone is privy to at any moment but rather an earned and achieved state of Buddhist Nirvana one has to “make” time to practice to be able to reap joy from the everday mundane.

It is as impossibly solvable as it is disheartening and semi-mature to realise that even the simple joy of sunbathing is granted only if you’re either the privileged or the mentally skewed who, with either the lack (or ability to afford the lack) of a suffering environment or the mental faculty to be aware of with, can feel the soft warmth of a glowing Sun on the many many folds of their skin with no figurative self-and-socially-imposed timer of success that labels this encounter with the Sun a loss of happiness.
For most the reality is what Silvio Dante says - Time is a great enemy.

I have been given a few “solutions” for this problem. The two prominent ones being either to compartmentalise my psyche or to, as mentioned before, practice achieving a state of Buddhist Nirvana before the office slack call begins

The latter is asinine. While the former is psychotic.
Asinine because instead of targeting the root problem it merely affords you bliss to bear it.(although it is hardly "mere")
Psychotic because like Nathaniel Hawthorne said -
No man can wear one face to himself and another to the multitude without finally getting bewildered as to which one is true.

Discounting the sheer resilience of practitioners of either of these solutions would not be wise. In the face of a travesity I aforementioned deemed “impossibly solvable” a fragment of pure inspiration (although whether it is rooted in rebellion or stupidity, i cannot discern) can be witnessed in these practitioners rebelling sisyphean odds with the entirety of both of their tiny hands

I, however, being a carrier of humble flesh and bones, find it equally impossibly difficult to averse myself from the despicable flawed system of society that asks me sacrifice before I can enjoy the warmth of the Sun. And so...

Through nothing I was formed. Through me nothing was formed.

Yours Truly,
The Nuke
